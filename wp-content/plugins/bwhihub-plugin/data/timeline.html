1. September 2013

    Brigham Digital Innovation Hub (iHub) Founded
    The iHub was founded from the first hackathon at Brigham and Women's Hospital.

  2. April 2014

    Innovation Series
    First Innovation Series (internal hackathon): Pulmonary and Critical Care.

  3. July 2014

    Digital Health Innovation Guide
    Launch of the Digital Health Innovation Guide.

  4. September 2014

    First Digital Health Pilot Launches
    With support from the iHub, Twine launches at Brigham Primary Care at South Huntington Clinic as a product validation study.

  5. July 2015

    Resident Rotation Program
    Hub starts two-week resident rotation programs for early innovator exposure and collaboration.

  6. April 2016

    iHub Shifts to Digital Focus
    iHub moves under Brigham CIO, Adam Landman, MD, shifting to 100% digital focus.

  7. August 2016

    First Brigham Mobile Application Launch
    iHub launches first Brigham branded mobile application.

  8. January 2017

    Startup Champion
    BWH joins MassChallenge HealthTech’s firt cohort as program champion.

  9. September 2017

    DHIG Process Published in Academic Medical Journal
    Case report titled “Catalyzing healthcare transformation with digital health: Performance indicators and lessons learned from a Digital Health Innovation Group” accepted and published by Elsevier.

  10. October 2017

    Wayfinding
    Launch of Wayfinding; a webbased navigation tool to help users find their way through the hospital.

  11. May 2018

    First Schlager Family Award
    First “Schlager Family Award for Early Stage Digital Health Innovation” granted, providing an opportunity to advance early stage projects from BWH doctors, researchers, and staff.

  12. June 2018

    Medumo Enterprise Agreement
    With the support of the iHub, Partners Healthcare signed a collaboration agreement with Medumo, an automated patient reminder platform, as their largest customer to date.

  13. September 2018

    iHub Turns 5
    Celebrating 5 years of innovation at an AMC, iHub takes a look back at the last 5 years with various panels and expos.

  14. July 2019

    Philips acquires Medumo
    Royal Philips, a global leader in health technology expanded its patient management solutions in the US with the acquisition of Boston-based start-up company Medumo, supported and piloted by the iHub.

  15. November 2019

    MeHi Sandbox Award
    The Massachusetts eHealth Institute at MassTech (MeHI) has selected the Brigham Digital Innovation Hub (iHub) to join the Digital Health Sandbox Network, which connects Massachusetts-based startups to cutting-edge R&D facilities and allows them the opportunity to apply for funding to test their innovations at one of the Network’s labs.
